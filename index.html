Is Elm dead?

No.

Elm development is happening in private. As Elm’s creator Evan Czaplicki wrote in February 2021: “If you like what you see now, that's pretty much what Elm is going to be for a while.”. In an update from November 2021, he shared some details about what he has been working on.
